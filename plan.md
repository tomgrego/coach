# Training Plan: The Path to 7a & Sub-50 10k

## Macrocycle 1: Transition to Performance (May - July 2026)
**Goal:** Establish the "Hardware" (Finger Strength/VO2 Max) and the "Floor" (Aerobic Base).
**Primary Objective:** September 10k Readiness.
**Secondary Objective:** Baseline for December 7a send.

---

## Mesocycle 1: Strength & Base Building (May 2026)
**Theme:** "Testing the Plumbing"
**Focus:** Finger recruitment, MCL stabilization, and establishing the 15km Zone 2 baseline.

### Weekly Structure (3:1 Periodization)
- **Weeks 1-3 (Build):** Increasing volume in Zone 2 running (10km -> 15km) and intensity in finger lifts (80% of 28kg max).
- **Week 4 (Deload):** 40% reduction in volume. Focus on movement fluidity and active recovery.

### Key Benchmarks
- **Climbing:** Consistent sends of 6a+/6b. No-hang lifts at 23kg (80% load).
- **Running:** Complete 15km Zone 2 run with <5% cardiac drift.
- **Strength:** Frenchies (3x3) with perfect form at 90/120 degrees.

---

## Mesocycle 2: Power Endurance & VO2 Max (June 2026)
**Theme:** "The Engine Upgrade"
**Focus:** Introducing 4x4 bouldering and increasing the intensity of Tuesday intervals.

### Weekly Structure
- **Mon:** Max Recruitment (6s lifts) + Frenchies.
- **Tue:** VO2 Max Intervals (4x1km at 4:45 min/km).
- **Wed:** Power Endurance (4x4 Bouldering) or 7- Redpoint attempts.
- **Fri:** Technical Bouldering (Modern slopers).
- **Sat:** 15km Zone 2 (Aerobic Base).

### Key Benchmarks
- **Climbing:** First 7- redpoint. 4x4 sets completed without total "stone forearm" failure.
- **Running:** Tuesday intervals at 4:45 pace feel "sustainable."

---

## Mesocycle 3: Specificity & Performance Prep (July 2026)
**Theme:** "Pace & Projecting"
**Focus:** 10k goal pace sustainability and Grade 7 technical mapping.

### Weekly Structure
- **Mon:** Maintenance Strength + Lock-off stability.
- **Tue:** 10k Goal Pace Intervals (3x2km at 5:00 min/km).
- **Wed:** Limit Projecting (Grade 7 attempts) + technical volume.
- **Sat:** 18km Zone 2 (Durability focus).

### Key Benchmarks
- **Climbing:** Grade 7 beta fully mapped; making links through the crux.
- **Running:** 5km "Tempo" run at 5:00 pace feels like a "Moderate" effort (Zone 4).

---

## Macrocycle 2: The September Peak (August - September 2026)
**Goal:** Sub-50 10k.
**Tactical Shift:** Running moves to high-intensity/low-volume. Climbing moves to maintenance to prevent CNS drain.

---

## Macrocycle 3: The Redpoint Peak (October - December 2026)
**Goal:** 7a Redpoint.
**Tactical Shift:** Running moves to pure recovery (Zone 2 only). Climbing moves to maximum power endurance and redpoint projecting on 7/7+ lines.

## Week 22 Detailed Workout Routines

### 🏋️ Monday: Max Strength & Structural Hardening
*   **Phase 1: Warm-up (10 Min)**
    *   Lunge with Spinal Twist: 5 reps/side.
    *   90/90 Hip Switch: 5 reps/side (open hips for stemming).
    *   Finger Prep: 3 sets of 10s light no-hang lifts (e.g. 5kg) to warm up tendons.
*   **Phase 2: Max Strength Finger Lifts (Deadlift Category)**
    *   **Deadlift / Quad Block Lifts:** 3 sets of 3 reps. Hold each lift for **6 seconds** using a **strict half-crimp**. Start set 1 at **20 kg** to test form. If form is perfect, step up to **21-23 kg**; if index finger sags/drifts, keep it at **20 kg** (auto-regulate). Rest 3 minutes between sets.
    *   *Coaching Cue:* Establish active shoulder pre-tension *before* you pull to mimic climbing physics. Ensure the index finger remains bent at 90° (no chisel drag).
*   **Phase 3: High-Intensity Power & Core (Rest 2-3 mins between sets)**
    *   **Frenchie Pull-ups:** 3 sets of 3-5 reps. (1 rep = Pull to top, hold 5s $\rightarrow$ lower to 90°, hold 5s $\rightarrow$ lower to 120°, hold 5s). Focus on the top hold. Squeeze pinkies.
    *   **Hanging Knee Raises:** 3 sets of 8-12 reps (auto-regulated based on form). Focus on keeping the lower back rounded and a slow 3-second negative descent. Squeeze your inner thighs together.
*   **Phase 4: Shoulder & Core Hardening Circuit (Rest 90s between exercises, repeat circuit 3 times)**
    *   **Scapular Pull-ups:** 12 reps (Keep arms dead straight, hold active squeeze for 2s at the top).
    *   **Banded Face Pulls (replaces Wrist Curls):** 15 reps. Pull band to nose, squeezing shoulder blades for a 2s hold at peak contraction. (Use a very light band, do not shrug).
    *   **Banded Hollow Body Holds (replaces Planks):** 45 seconds. Press lower back flat into the mat, arms and legs extended. Focus on maintaining a rigid hollow position.
    *   **Banded / Kettlebell IYTs (replaces Glute Bridges):** 10 reps of each letter (I, Y, T). Raise arms in control to form I, Y, and T letters. Targets lower trapezius and rotator cuff. (Use very light resistance).
    *   **Cossack Squats:** 10 total (Light hips mobility work and MCL tracking alignment).


### 🏃 Tuesday: VO2 Max Intervals
*   **Warm-up:** 10 mins easy jogging.
*   **Intervals:** 4 x 1km @ **4:45 min/km** pace. Rest 3 minutes (**active recovery: slow jogging/shuffling** to clear lactate via the skeletal muscle pump).
*   **Cool-down:** 5 mins easy walking.
*   *Safety Check:* RHR baseline limit is **55.0 bpm**. If morning RHR hits this, swap for a Recovery Run (HR < 135 bpm) or rest.

### 🧗 Wednesday: Power Endurance (4x4 Bouldering)
*   **Selection:** Pick 4 easy boulder problems (~V1-V2 or ~6a, well below limit) that you can climb cleanly.
*   **Execution:**
    *   Climb all 4 boulders back-to-back without resting (1 set, ~2-3 mins).
    *   Rest exactly **3 minutes** (ATP-CP replenishment).
    *   Repeat for **4 sets** total.
    *   *Cues:* Silent Feet (no sound on contact), Hover Point (1s pause over hold before grabbing).

### 🧘 Thursday: Thursday Mobility & Elbow Prehab (~35 mins)
*   **Warm-up (3 mins):** Cat-Cow Flow (10 cycles) then Child's Pose (60s). Inhale arching, exhale rounding.
*   **Elbow & Antagonist Prehab (12 mins):**
    *   **Tyler Twist:** 3x15 reps per arm (FlexBar eccentric lateral tendon load for tennis elbow).
    *   **Reverse Tyler Twist:** 3x15 reps per arm (FlexBar eccentric medial tendon load for golfer's elbow).
    *   **Forearm Pronation/Supination:** 3x15 reps each arm with light dumbbell (targets Pronator Teres).
    *   **Tendon Gliding Sequence:** 3 complete cycles per hand (hold each of the 5 positions for 5s).
    *   **Rubber Band Finger Extensions:** 3x15 reps (strengthens antagonists).
*   **Hips & Lower Body Mobility (7 mins):**
    *   **Quadruped Hip CARs:** 5 circles each direction, each hip (slow controlled, MCL-safe).
    *   **90/90 Hip Rotations:** 30s hold per side. Transitions back and forth. No knee forcing.
*   **Spine & Shoulder Mobility (8 mins):**
    *   **Bench T-Spine Mobilization:** 8 reps, hold end-range 5s (PVC palms-up).
    *   **Thoracic Windmill:** 8 reps each side (keep knee pinned on foam roller).
*   **Cooldown (5 mins):** Child's Pose 60s + Reclined Figure-Four 45s/side (MCL safe).
*   *Coaching Note:* Let your watch record the session for at least 35 minutes to ensure it registers toward the May Gains badge!

### 🧗 Friday: Technical Bouldering & Movement Economy
*   **Focus:** Core-to-wall skeletal weight transfer and movement efficiency. Keep HR strictly under **135 bpm** (active recovery, no interference with Saturday's run).
*   **Phase 1: Footwork Precision Warm-up (20 mins)**
    *   **Silent Feet & Sticky Feet:** Climb 3 easy warm-up routes. Lock eyes on each foothold until placement is completely silent and weighted. Zero micro-adjustments allowed.
*   **Phase 2: Hover Point Balance Drill (20 mins)**
    *   **Before & After Hovers:** Climb 2-3 vertical climbs. Hover your hand for 3 seconds directly over each target hold before gripping. Focus on locking off and stabilizing from the shoulder girdle and core.
*   **Phase 3: Sloth-Monkey Rhythm (20 mins)**
    *   Pick an easy vertical route and climb it twice:
        *   *Sloth:* Ultra-slow, smooth, completely reversible static movement.
        *   *Monkey:* High-tempo, dynamic movement utilizing hip momentum.

### 🏃 Saturday: Zone 2 Long Run
*   **Distance:** 15 km flat route.
*   **Pace:** Slow/conversational. HR must stay under **147 bpm** (Zone 2 compliance).
*   **Cadence:** Maintain **170-172 spm** for MCL joint protection.
*   **Hydration:** Drink 1L+ with electrolytes Friday night to prevent cardiac decoupling.

### 🧘 Sunday: Sunday Recovery & Elbow Prehab (~35 mins)
*   **Diaphragmatic Warm-up (4 mins):** Diaphragmatic Expansion Contrast (5 reps) + Sphinx to Baby Cobra (5 reps).
*   **Elbow & Tendon Prehab (7 mins):**
    *   **Tyler Twist & Reverse Tyler Twist:** 2x15 reps each per arm.
    *   **Tendon Gliding Sequence + Finger Extensions:** 2 full cycles / 2x15 reps.
*   **Posterior Chain Restoration (16 mins):**
    *   **Couch Stretch with Pelvic Tilt:** 60s each side (MCL safe: knee pointing straight forward).
    *   **Downward Dog with Pedaling:** 2 mins (restores calves, hamstrings, Achilles after Saturday's run).
    *   **Soleus Calf Stretch + Lying Hamstring Stretch:** 45s each side per stretch (MCL safe).
    *   **Reclined Figure-Four:** 60s each side (keep crossed foot flexed for MCL safety).
*   **Thoracic Mobility (3 mins):** Standing Thoracic Rotation (8 reps each side).
*   **Cooldown (5 mins):** Child's Pose 90s (deep breathing, body scan, weekly HRV/RHR review prep).
*   *Coaching Note:* Let your watch record the session for at least 35 minutes to ensure it registers toward the May Gains badge!
