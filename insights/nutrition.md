# Nutrition Insights for the 7a & 10k Goal

## Core Principles
- **Protein Intake:** Aim for **160g per day**. This is non-negotiable for repairing the structural damage from high-intensity climbing and long-distance running.
- **Caloric Budget:** ~2200-2300 kcal for weight loss phase (~0.5kg/week), aiming for 72-74kg race weight.
- **The "Potato" Rule:** Potatoes are a high-satiety carbohydrate source. Use them to manage hunger during the weight loss phase.
- **Hydration:** Critical for preventing "heavy legs" and cramp-related injury. Target 3L+ on training days, especially when running in heat.

## Periodization
- **High Carb Days:** Tuesday (Intervals) and Friday (Climbing). Fuel the high-intensity efforts.
- **Low Carb Days:** Thursday (Rest) and Sunday (Rest). Maintain the deficit without sacrificing performance.
- **Post-Workout:** Prioritize protein + moderate carbs within 2 hours of "Stone Forearm" sessions (climbing) or long runs.
